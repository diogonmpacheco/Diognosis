# MedCheck — Drug Interaction Checker

Free, private drug interaction checker. Enter your medications and see how they interact — no accounts, no data collection, everything runs in your browser.

**Live:** [diogonmpacheco.github.io/medcheck](https://diogonmpacheco.github.io/medcheck/)

## Features

- **247 drugs** — prescription, OTC, supplements, herbs, and recreational substances
- **Interaction engine** — enzyme inhibition/induction, QTc prolongation, serotonin syndrome, bleeding risk, and more
- **Metabolite cascades** — 104 drugs with full metabolite pathways and cross-referencing
- **Optional genetics** — set your CYP enzyme phenotypes for personalized drug level predictions
- **Clinical fold data** — AUC changes grounded in published pharmacokinetic studies
- **100% private** — single HTML file, no server, no tracking, no cookies

## How to Use

1. Visit the site or open `index.html` locally
2. Search or browse to add your medications
3. View interaction warnings, risk assessment, and metabolite details
4. Optionally set your genetics for more accurate predictions

## Disclaimer

This tool is for educational purposes only. It does not replace professional medical advice. Always consult your doctor or pharmacist before making changes to your medications.
